The world has changed. No one knows what brought about the big data.
Some say it’s social media. Some say it’s the birth of the Internet
But the world has changed ... FOREVER.
Our world has become a destination for data of all sorts, manifesting into the phenomenon mankind calls Big Data.
Mankind has entered a new age of technology, one where it’s very survival depends on mastering the old Internet with new Data Analysis.
There are many aimless souls drifting in the world of Big Data.
Lost presently in Facebook, cut off from the social networks of the past and facing the timeless treadmills of the Internet of Things future.
But, as with all of us, there comes a time when they must be awaken to their potential.
It’s time. R you with us?

The art of data analysis requires a certain set of skills, and the combination is powerful but rare.
